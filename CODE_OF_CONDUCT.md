# Code of Conduct

Poolstatis contributors are expected to keep collaboration technical, direct, and respectful.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas by their evidence and tradeoffs.
- Assume review comments are about the work, not the person.
- Keep issues and PRs focused on reproducible facts, concrete proposals, or scoped questions.
- Credit prior work and disclose conflicts of interest when relevant.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Posting secrets, customer data, or exploit details in public threads.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or PRs after maintainers ask to move on.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or
decline contributions that violate this code. Security issues should follow
`SECURITY.md`, not public debate.
